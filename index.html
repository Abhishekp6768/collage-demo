About SRU Raipur

Excellence in Education Since 2005

Our University

Shri Rawatpura Sarkar University (SRU), Raipur is a premier institution of higher education established in 2005 under the Chhattisgarh Private Universities Act. With a sprawling 50-acre campus, SRU offers world-class education across diverse disciplines.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in education, research, and innovation that transforms students into socially responsible leaders and change-makers.

Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge research, and industry partnerships that foster intellectual growth and professional development.

Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Fill online application form with required documents. Application fee: 1000

Step 2: Entrance Test

Appear for SRU Entrance Exam (SRUEE) or submit valid national test scores.

Step 3: Interview

Personal interview for shortlisted candidates (online/offline options).

Step 4: Admission

Fee payment and document verification for final admission.

Fee Structure & Scholarships

Fee Structure (Annual)

  • B.Tech: 1,25,000
  • MBA: 1,50,000
  • BBA: 75,000
  • M.Tech: 1,00,000
  • Other UG Programs: 50,000 - 80,000

Scholarships Available

  • Merit Scholarship: Up to 100% tuition fee waiver
  • Sports Scholarship: Up to 50% fee waiver
  • Girl Child Scholarship: 25% fee waiver
  • Economically Weaker Section: Up to 75% waiver
  • Special Talent Scholarship: Case-by-case basis
